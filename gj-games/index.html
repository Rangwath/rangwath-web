Here, you'll find a collection of games created with a small team of friends during various game jams. Each game represents our shared enthusiasm for collaborative game development. Take a moment to explore our creations and enjoy the fruits of our teamwork and creativity.

Tidy Teddy, Messy Teddy (2023)

Introducing our latest creation developed during our second thrilling three-day game jam in Brno, where our team of three enthusiasts once again came together. Embracing the theme "That one room", our game invites players into a 3D platformer adventure, crafted with custom shaders to enhance the visual experience.

Please note that due to the use of custom shaders, the game is only available for download, as the Unity WebGL platform does not support used shaders.

Need for Jamf (2022)

Introducing our special creation crafted during the exhilarating 16-hour company Hacksgiving event in Brno, where a friendly team of four colleagues joined forces. We embarked on a mission to craft a small multiplayer (split-screen) 3D racing game, designed to provide our colleagues with a relaxing and enjoyable experience.

Utilizing the stunning Office Pack assets by Synty Studios, we transformed our company's office into a racing track for RC cars.

Eat Gun (2022)

Introducing our collaborative creation born during a lively three-day game jam in Brno, where a team of three friends came together. Embracing the theme "A gun you cannot shoot", our short 2D platformer captures the essence of creativity and camaraderie that defined the jamming experience.

While this version represents my efforts to refine the original game jam prototype with a few bug fixes and an online leaderboard, it stays true to our initial vision. It serves as a reminder of the fun and innovative potential that emerged during the intense yet rewarding game jam.
